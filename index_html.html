Pyramid Energy Hypothesis

This project proposes a new integrative hypothesis in cancer therapy: that the precise geometry of pyramid structures may influence cellular energy balance. The hypothesis is supported by preliminary research on seed germination, tumor-bearing rats, and early-stage patient trials.

Mohammed Farahat, an independent scientific researcher, introduces this bilingual initiative to raise awareness and invite support for further experimental validation.
